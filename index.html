Privacy Policy

Application: Fadel Moto Parts

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fadel Moto Parts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Phone number (if you register using your phone number)
  • Google account information (when signing in with Google)

Motorcycle Information (Optional)

  • Brand
  • Model
  • Year

This information is optional and is used to help identify compatible motorcycle parts.

Order Information

  • Delivery phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Purchased products
  • Order history
  • Payment verification information (Whish Money transaction reference)

Wishlist

Products you choose to save to your wishlist.

Device Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate your identity.
  • Process and manage your orders.
  • Deliver products.
  • Recommend compatible motorcycle parts.
  • Maintain your wishlist.
  • Send order updates.
  • Send security verification codes.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve application performance.
  • Send important notifications.

4. Authentication

You may sign in using:

  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Google Sign-In

Google Sign-In is verified by our backend services using Google's identity token before creating or accessing your account.

5. Payment Information

Payments are verified using Whish Money. We do not store your banking information or credit/debit card information. Only the payment reference required to verify your payment may be stored.

6. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send:

  • Order updates
  • Account notifications
  • Service announcements

7. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)
  • Google Sign-In
  • MailerSend (Email verification)

Additional third-party services may be added in future updates. This Privacy Policy will be updated whenever our data practices change.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

10. Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated personal information, please contact us at fadelmotoparts@gmail.com. We will process eligible requests in accordance with applicable laws and our internal procedures.

11. Children's Privacy

Fadel Moto Parts is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
